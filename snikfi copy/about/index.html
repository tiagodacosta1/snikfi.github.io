About

Hi there, my name is Fiz, known online as snikfi.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and entrepreneur with experience in the fashion, music, virtual reality and software development industries. I'm currently employed by Mendix as a UX/UI designer. Mendix is a low-code platform that provides tools to build, test, deploy and iterate applications.

I have worked with innovative companies such as Cyberdog, a UK Cult Fashion brand & Kitayah Music, a music production company with a wide variety of high-end musical connections.

I studied BA (Hons) Information & Interface Design at University of the Arts London, London College of Communication before studying MSc User Experience Engineering at Goldsmiths, University of London. My academic work investigates services that assist neurodivergent users in completing actions through the ever-increasing digital domain.

My most recent piece of academic work was Abstraction against Comprehension, my Master's thesis, which investigated visual abstraction within interface design and its effect on neurodivergent users. I'm interested in discovering issues for users with disabilities and communicating solutions in the most tailored way I can.
